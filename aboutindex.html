THE GUIDING QUESTION
To what extent is inclusion motivated in data science education?

A BRIEF BACKGROUND
This ethnographic study is a part of the Berkeley Institue of Data Science (BIDS) as well as a larger initiative to make data science more accessible to undergraduates.
Since there are no explicit undergraduate classes on data science, this study observes a course that boasted of data science pedagogy to better understand the makings of an effective + inclusive curriculum to further inform the undergraduate initiative.
