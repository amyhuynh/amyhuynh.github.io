A BRIEF BACKGROUND
what is social good?
Contemporary ethicist, John Rawls, defines it to be "certain general conditions that are...equally to everyone's advantage."
This is inherently releated to the concept of social justice which, as defined by John T. Jost and Aaron C. Kay can be summed up by three basic conditions: "1. benefits and burdens in society are dispersed in accordance with some allocation principle (or set of principles); 2. procedures, norms, and rules that govern political and other forms of decision making preserve the basic rights, liberties, and entitlements of individuals and groups; and 3. human beings are treated with dignity and respect not only by authorities but also by other relevant social actors, including fellow citizens."

what is good science?
Good science is the pursuit of the truth. Good science understands the data that is being inputted into the experiment, is not pressured by the need to publish, follows the scientific method, is peer-reviewed + reproducible.
the site of study Over the course of a semester (15 weeks), I have observed a graduate level data science course held in Li-Ka Shing.
PROJECT SPONSOR
This ethnographic study is a part of the Berkeley Institue of Data Science (BIDS) as well as a larger initiative to make data science more accessible to undergraduates.
Since there are no explicit undergraduate classes on data science, this study observes a course that boasted of data science pedagogy to better understand the makings of an effective + inclusive curriculum to further inform the undergraduate initiative.
